| Session | Time (Bangkok) | Description |
|---------|----------------|-------------|

## Trading Lot

- **Board Lot:** 100 shares
- **Odd Lot:** < 100 shares (traded on odd-lot board)

---

## Short Selling Rules

1. Only allowed for designated securities
2. Must borrow shares through SBL (Securities Borrowing & Lending)
3. Uptick rule applies (must sell at price ≥ last trade)
4. Maximum short position: 10% of paid-up capital
5. Daily short selling report required

---

## Settlement

- **Settlement Cycle:** T+2
- **Currency:** Thai Baht (THB)
- **Clearing House:** Thailand Clearing House (TCH)
