## Company Overview

Bangkok Bank is one of Thailand's largest commercial banks with over 80 years of history. The bank has an extensive regional network across ASEAN and Greater China, serving both corporate and retail customers.

## Investment Thesis

### Bull Case
1. **Improving Net Interest Margin (NIM)** as rates normalize
2. **Strong asset quality** with NPL ratio below industry average
3. **Regional expansion** driving fee income growth
4. **Digital banking initiatives** improving cost efficiency

### Key Risks
1. Economic slowdown affecting loan growth
2. Rising NPLs in SME segment
3. Intense competition in digital banking
4. Regulatory capital requirements
