## Company Overview

Delta Electronics (Thailand) is a leading power electronics manufacturer, producing power supplies, cooling systems, and EV components. The company is a key beneficiary of global AI infrastructure buildout and electric vehicle adoption.

## Investment Thesis

### Bull Case
1. **AI datacenter boom** driving demand for power supplies
2. **EV component orders** from major automakers
3. **Strong backlog** extending into 2027
4. **Margin expansion** from product mix improvement

### Key Risks
1. Customer concentration risk
2. Currency fluctuation (THB/USD)
3. Supply chain disruptions
4. Technology obsolescence
