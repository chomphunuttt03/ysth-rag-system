## Company Overview

Kasikornbank (KBANK) is Thailand's fourth-largest bank by assets. Known for its strong digital banking platform K PLUS, KBANK serves over 20 million digital banking customers.

## Investment Thesis

### Positives
1. **Market leader in digital banking** with K PLUS super app
2. **Strong SME lending franchise**
3. **Growing wealth management business**
4. **Good cost control** initiatives

### Concerns
1. Higher NPL ratio compared to peers at **3.8%**
2. Slower loan growth in corporate segment
3. Margin pressure from deposit competition
4. Elevated credit costs
