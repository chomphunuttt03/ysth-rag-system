## Company Overview

PTT Public Company Limited is Thailand's national petroleum and natural gas company. It is the largest company listed on the Stock Exchange of Thailand by market capitalization. PTT operates across the entire petroleum value chain including exploration, production, refining, and retail.

## Investment Thesis

### Bull Case
1. **Strong refining margins** expected to continue through 2026
2. **LNG business expansion** with new terminal capacity coming online
3. **EV charging network** growth through partnerships
4. **Stable dividend yield** of 4.5% provides downside protection

### Key Risks
1. Oil price volatility affecting upstream earnings
2. Energy transition pressure on fossil fuel demand
3. Regulatory changes in energy pricing
4. Competition in retail fuel business
